Supplements & Hydration

Daily Supplements

  • • Omega-3: Take once daily with a meal
  • • Vitamin C: Take once daily after lunch
  • • Whey Protein: As needed for protein goals

Hydration Guidelines

  • • Minimum 2.5 liters of water daily
  • • Avoid acidic or spicy drinks
  • • Monitor hydration levels
  • • Increase intake during workouts

Key Nutritional Strategies

Meal Preparation Guidelines

  • • Focus on gentle cooking methods (steaming, grilling)
  • • Prepare meals in advance when possible
  • • Store meals properly in airtight containers
  • • Keep ingredients fresh and high-quality

Performance Optimization

  • • Maintain consistent sleep schedule
  • • Listen to your body's hunger signals
  • • Adjust portions based on activity level
  • • Stay consistent with meal timing
